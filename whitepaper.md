Our vision paper **Decentralized Intelligence Network (DIN)** was selected from over **100+ speaker applications** to be showcased at the **[Summit on Responsible Decentralized Intelligence: The Future of Decentralization and AI](https://rdi.berkeley.edu/events/decentralizationaisummit24)**, hosted by Berkeley RDI on August 6, 2024, at the Verizon Center, Cornell Tech Campus, Roosevelt Island, NYC. This prestigious event featured contributions from some of the most influential organizations and leaders in the fields of AI and decentralization including **NVIDIA**, **Google**, **Oasis Labs Founder**, and **Worldcoin Foundation** to name a few.
