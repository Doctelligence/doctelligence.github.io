Dr. Abraham Nash is a DPhil student in Computer Science at Oxford University, exploring data sovereignty, decentralized AI (DeAI), plurality, public goods, and blockchain-aligned ideals.

As the author of the vision paper for the Decentralized Intelligence Network (DIN), Abraham’s work at Doctelligence contributes to shaping a new era of AI—one that is open, decentralized, and focused on preserving individual data sovereignty. He is dedicated to supporting the lab’s mission to advance AI through innovative and privacy-respecting technologies.
