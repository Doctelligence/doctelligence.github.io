This roadmap outlines the strategic phases for developing the network, focusing on building a robust decentralized AI ecosystem while ensuring data privacy, security, and community engagement.

## Phase 1: Foundation & Initial Development

### 📅 Oxford Team Building
- Establish a strong, cohesive team within Oxford’s CS group, leveraging our established peer network to ensure a solid foundation. (✓)

### 📄 Whitepaper Launch
- Refine and expand the Whitepaper to incorporate the latest advancements in the protocol and ecosystem. (✓)

### 📅 Presentation at RDI Berkeley (NYC) DeAI Event
- Present at RDI Berkeley and key NYC events, engaging with leaders in the DeAI and blockchain ecosystems. (✓)
  
### 🧪 Prototype & Experiments (Live)
- Deploy live prototypes and conduct experiments on advanced computing clusters, including on-chain unit tests and artifact construction to optimize network performance. (✓)

### 👥 Team Hiring & Execution Strategy
- Develop a clear strategy for core team recruitment, positioning Testnet protocol development for success with expert input. (✓)

### 💰 Seed Funding (Year 0)
- Secure seed funding from VCs whose investment thesis aligns with our mission. (-)

### 🌐 Strategic Ecosystem Expansion
- Forge key partnerships with Oxford AI & Blockchain Hubs, Berkeley DeAI, Innovation Centres, and others to expand the ecosystem. (-)

## Phase 3: Testnet Launch & Developer Ecosystem

### 🚀 Testnet Deployment & Feedback Integration
- Live Testnet launch and gather actionable feedback from validators, AI developers, and data ecosystem partners to refine the network. (-)

### 🧑‍💻 AI Developer Tools & Data Integration
- Roll out APIs, prebuilt models, and SDKs to streamline onboarding and enable rapid data integration (e.g., SOLID). (-)

### 🔧 Protocol Optimization & Security Auditing
- Continuously optimize protocol performance, conduct regular security audits, and perform penetration testing to ensure system robustness. (-)

### 🔗 Staking Ecosystem Launch
- Initiate validator staking and conduct network testing to develop the **Delegated Proof of Stake (DPoS)** ecosystem, ensuring decentralization and security. (-)

### 🌍 Ecosystem Expansion & Strategic Partnerships
- Accelerate ecosystem growth through strategic partnerships (e.g., **Google DeepMind**), **Oxford-based Encode Club hackathons**, and key grants to fuel development and align decentralized ecosystems. (-)

### 🎉 Genesis Token Airdrop
- Distribute tokens to early Testnet participants, including validators, data holders, AI developers, and community delegators, to fuel decentralized network growth. (-)

## Phase 4: Mainnet Deployment & Decentralization

### 🖥️ Mainnet Deployment
- Launch the **Doctelligence** Mainnet, establishing a decentralized public infrastructure that generates network fees with staking capabilities to support the ecosystem and drive long-term value. (-)

### 💸 Foundation & Development Funds Management
- Strategically manage token development funds to foster ecosystem growth, supporting blockchain infrastructure, application development, and network maturity through transparent, decentralized resource allocation. (-)

### 🏛️ Decentralization of Governance & Ownership
- Transition governance and ownership to the decentralized community, setting the network on course as public infrastructure enabling developer contribution and participant engagement. (-)

### 🧑‍🔬 Doctelligence Labs – AI for Decentralized Networks
- Shift lab to developing advanced AI technologies, tools, and enterprise solutions, while raising capital to accelerate innovation and expansion in the DeAI sector. (-)

### ⚡ Launch of 'Layer AI' Blockchain Network
- Advance Validators' role to launch a **Layer 2 blockchain network**, **'Layer AI'**, supporting scalable decentralized infrastructure for AI and enterprise applications. (-)

### 🌍 Real-World DApps & Enterprise Adoption
- Drive the adoption of real-world DApps across industries, including **DePIN**, **DeFi**, **Healthcare**, and **Smart Cities**. (-)
